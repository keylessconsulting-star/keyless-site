Security

Three Layers of Security. Zero Extra Cost.

Layer 1: The Vehicle

The lockbox mounts to the window glass. The car's own door lock becomes the first security layer. Can't reach the box without unlocking the car.

Layer 2: The Lockbox

Sealed aluminum shell. No visible seams. No pry points. Hardened steel latch. Faraday cage blocks key fob relay attacks.

Layer 3: The Encryption

AES-128 rolling codes. Same encryption used by banks. New challenge every session. Replay attacks impossible.

The car protects the box. The box protects the key. The key starts the car.
